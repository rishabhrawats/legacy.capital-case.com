What’s inside Velocity.

What actually happens in Founder OS Velocity?

Velocity is a 12-month execution program for founders who already have traction and want better systems, better decision-making, and better support. It’s not an event, and it’s not a course. It’s a structured operating environment.

You join weekly live sessions led by Matt and members of his executive team (product, operations, distribution, content, strategy). These sessions break down the same systems we use internally at Founder OS - operating rhythms, team structures, content systems, monetization paths, and decision frameworks.

Nothing is promised or guaranteed. Your implementation determines your results. Velocity simply gives you the structure, support, community, access to Matt, and clarity to run your business with more intention.

What do I walk away with?

Most founders walk away with a clearer operating plan, better weekly rhythm, and the next 5-10 systems to install.

You work directly with our team to tighten execution, clean up operations, and build a content engine that suits your model.

You’ll have ongoing support, a phase-mapped plan, and a community to help you stay accountable.

Outcomes vary. We don’t guarantee revenue, results, or growth. We focus on improving how you operate so you can make better decisions with more clarity.

Is this right for my business size/industry?

Velocity is designed for founders doing $30K–$500K/month who want better systems, cleaner operations, and a more intentional way to scale.

We work across many industries - coaching, consulting, agencies, SaaS, education, and digital products.

Fit depends more on your stage than your niche: you already have traction, and you want support to run things more effectively.

If you’re below $30K/month or still finding product-market fit, Velocity likely isn’t the right step yet. We have other offers to help you if you’re doing less than $30K/month. You can book a call below to learn more.
